Social Bingo

Connect with people around you. Answer fun questions. Get five in a row. Win.

What is it?

Social bingo—a game to connect people at events via questions.

Why Bingo?

Bingo is familiar, engaging, and fun. Perfect for breaking the ice.

Perfect for...

Student mixers. Networking events. Team building. Community gatherings.

①

Find People

Read questions on your card. Find people who match them.

②

Tap a Square

When you find someone matching a question, tap their square.

③

Get Bingo

Connect 5 in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal). You win!
